🌸Favourite Food🌸

One of my favourite food is yong tau foo. I can enjoy a variety of foods in one bowl such as sausages, crab fillet, shrimp fillet, quail eggs and more. The thicker the sauces, the tastier the yong tau foo will be.

🌸Favourite Drink🌸

For drink, I will always go with matcha green tea. Since it contains caffeine, it can boost up my energy because unlike many other health teas that require countless teaspoons of sugar to make it drinkable, matcha is absolutely wonderful on its own. 😍
